## Verbatim excerpt (`Basel POR TP-1`)

> Banks should manage their dependencies on relationships, including those of, but not
> limited to, third parties or intragroup entities, for the delivery of critical
> operations.

Supporting text (para 31, same section, available if a fuller quote is wanted):

> Banks should perform a risk assessment and due diligence before entering into
> arrangements including those of, but not limited to, third parties or intragroup
> entities, consistent with the bank's operational risk management framework,
> outsourcing/third-party risk management policy and operational resilience approach.

**Why it matters (RMiT 17.1):** the international baseline keeps responsibility for
third-party (incl. cloud) dependencies with the bank whatever the approval model — so
17.1's shift from consultation to 14-day notification must preserve that responsibility.
