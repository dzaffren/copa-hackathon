What you might have missed

Connections a human scouring source-by-source would likely miss.

The AI reads across every connected source at once — so it surfaces cross-source implications, silent gaps, second-order links, and temporal divergences (vs what we said last year) you wouldn't spot reading one document at a time. These are AI-generated hypotheses to verify, not asserted facts — each names the sources it reasoned across and carries a confidence band so you know what to check first. You decide what to act on: carry it into the redraft assistant, or set it aside.
