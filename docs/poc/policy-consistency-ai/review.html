BANK NEGARA MALAYSIA · RMiT (2026 draft)

17 Consultation and notification related to cloud services

17.1 A financial institution shall notify the Bank within 14 days of the first-time adoption of a public cloud for critical systems. (Prior consultation replaced with post-adoption notification to speed up cloud adoption.)

17.2 A financial institution shall notify the Bank on any subsequent adoption of a public cloud for a critical system.

🔍 What changed

Clause 17.1 shifts first-time public-cloud adoption from prior consultation with the Bank to 14-day post-adoption notification. This is a substantive control change, not just wording.

Rulebook Radar will now scan the linked policies for any clause that assumes the old "consult before adopting" rule — starting with the Outsourcing policy's approval requirement.
