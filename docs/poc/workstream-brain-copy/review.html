Aisyah R. · edited 2h ago
§4.4 · Dependency mapping

A financial institution shall map dependencies for each critical business service and refresh the mapping at least annually.

§5.3 · Scenario testing

A financial institution shall conduct scenario testing annually, covering severe-but-plausible disruptions to critical business services.

§6.3 · Responsibility mapping

The board shall assign a single senior officer, accountable for operational resilience outcomes across all critical business services.

§7.1 · Technology risk anchor

Financial institutions shall comply with the Policy Document on Risk Management in Technology issued on 1 June 2023 when establishing controls for technology-enabled operations.

§8.2 · Third-party concentration

Where more than one financial institution shares a technology service provider providing a critical business service, the Bank may impose additional oversight requirements.

Front matter

This Policy Document supersedes the version issued on 28 November 2025. All references to the 1 June 2023 version shall be construed as references to this Document.

§10.49 · Board approval

The board must approve the technology risk appetite at least annually.

§17.1 · Third-party technology risk

A financial institution shall identify, assess and manage risks arising from the use of third-party technology service providers, including cloud service providers.

§17.2 · Critical technology outsourcing

Where a critical technology service is outsourced, the financial institution remains fully responsible for outcomes affecting operational resilience.

§21.5 · Scenario testing (tech)

A financial institution shall conduct scenario testing on technology recovery capabilities not less than once every calendar year.
